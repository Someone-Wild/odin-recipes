Feta and Tomato Pasta

This fresh and aromatic dish is sure to become a staple of your at home cooking.

This recipe is one of those go to's for me. It is sure to make the whole house smell good while cooking, and that only makes the end result that much more enjoyable. The combonation of feta, cherry tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil always makes a house smell alive. Fresh ingredients are best, but even on a tighter budget this dish is sure to make your mouth water!

This may not appear to be the healthiest dish ever, and I'll be honest, its not the best but it sure is close enough. Depending on your pasta choice the whole dish can be gluten free on top of it already being vegetarian. It is a very simple dish to scale up or down depending on how many poeple are going to be enjoying this dish. This feeds more than you might realize, so be prepared to pack some away for the next few days.

Ingredients

  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
