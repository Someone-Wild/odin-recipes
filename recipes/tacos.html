Beef Tacos

Great easy dish sure to feed the whole house

This is great to give out to dinner parties. Loads of options for food to be made

You can make this vegan, vegetariean, or full of meat

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Your favorite seasoning packet
  • Taco shells or tortillas

Steps

  1. Brown and season beef
  2. Prep all toppings
  3. Set out all options
  4. Serve
